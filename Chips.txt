1. Heat up air fryer or regular fryer to 350 deg cel.
2. Place 2 tortillas ontop of each other with a layer of olvie oil, pepper
   , salt, spice, and red pepper in between the two.
3. Rub the two together till both are lathered top and bottom.
4. Cut up tortilla into chips shape or any shape of your liking and plop them into the fryer for 
   5-10 mins depending on how you want the chips, do a small amount at a time not all at once.
